WHY STARTUP GAME?

  • Reasons given.
  • Tool for examination.

It is designed to motivate our brightest ,most creative and hardest working individuals to improve the use of society's resources.

THE GAME!

  1. Exam in business.
  2. Ventures in business.

It begins with an aspiring entrepreneur who is willing to step right up and be tested.The player starts with an artificial currency in this case the stock of new venture.


THE DEAL

The mordern is the highest form of human corporation.
Specialized resources in the form of labour ,raw and finished materials ,capital and knowlegde come together in a process that transforms these components into goods and services.Business evolve overtime ,as less efficient corporations are replaced by more efficient ones ,whose successful practices are then emulated by others.
Due to this the startup game developed.


THE GOAL!

The goal is to increase the value of the entrepreneur's shares because when the game is over these can be cashed for real money .

SWEAT EQUITY.

When employees get a chance to participate in the game and pay for it with their own labour.
The entrepreneur doesn't raise all the required money .
